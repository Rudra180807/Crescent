Our Story

Founded in 1985 by Chef Ahmed Al-Mansour, Crescent Restaurant began as a small family-owned eatery with a passion for authentic Mediterranean cuisine. What started as a humble kitchen serving traditional dishes to the local community has grown into one of the most beloved restaurants in the city.

Our philosophy is simple: use the freshest ingredients, prepare them with time-honored techniques, and serve them with genuine hospitality. Many of our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean.

Today, under the leadership of Chef Al-Mansour's daughter, Leila, we continue to honor our roots while innovating to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

Our Chef

Chef Leila Al-Mansour brings over 20 years of culinary expertise to Crescent Restaurant. Trained in both traditional Mediterranean cooking and modern culinary techniques, Chef Leila creates dishes that honor tradition while appealing to contemporary palates.

"Food is more than sustenance - it's a way to bring people together, to share culture, and to create memories. That's what we strive for every day at Crescent," says Chef Leila.
