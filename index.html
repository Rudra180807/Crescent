Discover Our Story

For over three decades, Crescent Restaurant has been a landmark of exceptional Mediterranean cuisine.

Founded in 1985 by Chef Ahmed Al-Mansour, we combine traditional recipes with contemporary culinary artistry. Our passion for authentic flavors and commitment to quality have made us a beloved destination for both locals and visitors alike.
